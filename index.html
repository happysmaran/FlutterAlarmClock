Flutter Alarm Clock Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 14 March 2025

Thank you for using Flutter Alarm Clock! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. No Data Collection

Flutter Alarm Clock does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any information to us or third parties.

2. Storage Permission

The app requires storage permission to allow you to select a media file (e.g., a song or sound) from your device to use as your alarm tone. This permission is used solely for this purpose and does not involve accessing or sharing any other data on your device.

3. No Internet Access

Flutter Alarm Clock does not connect to the internet. All functionality is performed locally on your device, ensuring your data remains private and secure.

4. Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the app's functionality, please feel free to contact us at saratbinduvallabhaneni@gmail.com.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

Thank you for trusting Flutter Alarm Clock!
